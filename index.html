About Us

Moms in Project Management (MiPM) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering mothers with Project Management education, mentorship, and career support. We believe that motherhood builds powerful skills—planning, budgeting, scheduling, problem-solving—that deserve recognition in the workforce.

Our Core Programs

Education & Certification

Foundational courses and certification support (PMP, CAPM, Agile, Scrum) with affordable access to study materials.

Mentorship & Coaching

Peer mentorship circles and access to professional project managers for personalized guidance and career coaching.

Job Readiness

Resume building, interview prep, and direct pipelines to employers through returnships and full-time roles.

Community & Advocacy

An online support network, monthly webinars, events, and advocacy for inclusive and flexible workplaces.

Introducing Agile Mother Labs

A groundbreaking initiative where mothers re-entering the workforce practice Agile and Scrum in real-world sprint environments. Through partnerships with startups and nonprofits, moms gain hands-on experience, peer support, and portfolio-ready evidence of their leadership and skills.
