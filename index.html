The Challenge

Millions of Moms Are Ready. The System Isn't.

Women step away from careers to raise families, and when they're ready to return, the doors feel closed. They're told they're "out of touch" — yet they manage complex, high-stakes projects every single day.

Career Gaps Penalized

Mothers returning to work face bias and outdated hiring practices that overlook their transferable skills.

Untapped Potential

Moms manage budgets, schedules, stakeholders, and crises daily — skills that map directly to project management.

Confidence Gap

Many feel left behind, underqualified, and invisible — even though they're more capable than they realize.

The Opportunity

Why Project Management?

A fast-growing field where the skills of motherhood become superpowers.

01

Booming Industry

Project management is one of the fastest-growing career fields globally, with demand outpacing supply across every sector.

02

Skills Already There

Moms already have the core competencies — time management, stakeholder coordination, risk mitigation. We just bridge the gap with formal training.

03

Motherhood = Leadership

We're reinventing motherhood as leadership training, turning lived experience into recognized professional qualifications.

Our Mission

Empowering Mothers. Building Careers. Transforming Communities.

To empower mothers through project management education and support, enabling them to enter or re-enter the workforce with confidence and contribute meaningfully to the economy.

"Because Every Mom is a Project Manager"

Our Impact Goals

Building the First PM Career Ecosystem for Mothers

Our ambitious 5-year roadmap is designed to create lasting systemic change for mothers everywhere.

1

10,000 Moms Empowered

Training and supporting ten thousand mothers over five years to launch PM careers.

2

1,000 Certifications

Securing PMP and CAPM certifications for one thousand moms in the next two years.

3

50+ Partner Organizations

Building a network of employers committed to returnship and hiring programs for mothers.

4

First-of-Its-Kind Ecosystem

Creating the world's first PM career support ecosystem designed specifically for mothers.
