Our Story

From One WhatsApp Broadcast to a Movement

MIPM started with a simple question: what if moms could get project management certified together, for free? One WhatsApp post. Fifteen moms who said "I'm in." A WhatsApp group that buzzes every single day. That's how movements start — not with a business plan, but with a group of women who decided to bet on themselves.

Our Mission

To empower mothers through project management education and support, enabling them to enter or re-enter the workforce with confidence.

The Program

12 Weeks to Your CAPM Certification

A structured, free study program designed around a mother's schedule

What's Included

Google PM Certificate

📷

Weekly Group Calls

45-minute video check-ins every week for accountability, questions, and genuine support.

✎

Free Practice Exams

700+ questions across PMLearning, Mometrix, and Agilemania. All free, all curated for you.

WhatsApp Community

Daily support from moms who get it. Ask questions, share wins, and keep each other going.

Flexible Study Schedule

5 hours per week, self-paced within weekly goals. Designed to fit around nap times and school runs.
